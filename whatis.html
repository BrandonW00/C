What is a diesel motor

Diesel motors are motors that have a much higher torque output than a gas motor. This is caused do to the internal combustion at a high temperature while diesel fuel is injected to the cylinder where combustion and expansion actuate as a piston.
Diesel motors are mostly used for towing. Due to their high torque output it is easy for them to pull mass amounts of weight.

There is big diesel motors like the CAT 6nz that is used in semis and there is smaller motors like the cummins 6.7 used in Ram pickups.

Why they are important:

They can tow heavy objects

More fuel efficient than gas motors

Can last a lot longer than a gas motor
